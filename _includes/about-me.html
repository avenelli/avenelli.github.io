I am a cryptography engineer currently working in Thalès Communications & Security since July 2014.

I have a Ph.D. in Computer Science of the Université de la Méditerranée, with Alexis Bonnecaze as supervisor at the Ecole Supérieure d'Ingénieurs de Luminy, from February 2008 to January 2011 with Vincent Dupaquis as my scientific advisor in Inside Secure.

My main research interests are:

  • Physical security of embedded cryptosystems: side-channel attacks (SPA, DPA, templates), fault attacks (laser, EM) and analysis of efficient countermeasures;
  • Security analysis and vulnerability assessment of embedded devices (smart cards and SoCs);
  • Machine learning and deep learning
